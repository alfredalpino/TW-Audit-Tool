# Backlink Audit and Outreach Plan

## Current Snapshot (from SEOptimer audit)

- Referring domains: 3
- Total backlinks: 3
- Dofollow backlinks: 0
- Nofollow backlinks: 3
- Authority quality: weak

## Immediate Backlink Audit Actions

1. Export backlinks from Google Search Console + one secondary tool.
2. Tag every referring domain with:
   - relevance (high/medium/low)
   - anchor quality (brand/topical/spammy)
   - risk level (safe/watch/disavow-candidate)
3. Build a disavow candidate sheet for links with foreign-language spam anchors or irrelevant indexing pages.
4. Keep a 14-day recheck before submitting disavow to avoid false positives.

## Priority Outreach Targets (first 10)

These are starting categories to secure higher-relevance links fast:

1. Clutch profile optimization + backlink to homepage and service page.
2. GoodFirms profile with service-level links.
3. DesignRush agency listing.
4. Founder-focused guest post on Indie Hackers-style publication.
5. SaaS or startup newsletter contribution with case-based insight.
6. Local UP business network or chamber directory listing.
7. Marketing operations community post with branded link.
8. Technical SEO case contribution on niche SEO publication.
9. Development-focused article on a web engineering community blog.
10. Partnership spotlight with complementary agency/tool provider.

## Anchor Text Policy

- 70% branded: `Torpedo Web`, `torpedoweb.org`
- 20% mixed topical: `web development and growth systems`
- 10% exact-match intent anchors max
- Avoid repetitive exact-match anchors.

## Outreach Cadence

- Day 1: finalize list and contact details.
- Day 2: send first 10 personalized outreach emails.
- Day 4: first follow-up for non-responders.
- Day 7: second follow-up + replacements for non-responsive prospects.

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least 10 qualified outreach conversations started.
- At least 3 links committed or published in first cycle.
- All new links logged with source, anchor, target page, and publish date.
