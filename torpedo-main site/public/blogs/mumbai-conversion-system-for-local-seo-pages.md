# Mumbai Conversion System for Local SEO Pages

High-intent traffic in Mumbai is expensive to win and easy to waste without a conversion system.

## System components

- City intent page mapped to one primary keyword.
- Proof block with local relevance and outcomes.
- FAQ that addresses buying objections.
- CTA that sets a clear next step.

## Metrics

Track impressions, CTR, qualified form submissions, and call booking rate by page. Optimize in that order.
