# Bengaluru SaaS Local SEO Mistakes Founders Make

Bengaluru SaaS pages often rank for broad terms but miss bottom-funnel local intent.

## Common mistakes

- Over-indexing on thought leadership, under-indexing on commercial pages.
- Missing comparison and buyer-intent language.
- Weak internal links between service and location pages.
- No structured experimentation loop.

## Corrective plan

1. Build commercial local pages for agency/developer/SEO intents.
2. Improve semantic depth and local trust proof.
3. Connect support blogs to money pages with explicit anchors.
