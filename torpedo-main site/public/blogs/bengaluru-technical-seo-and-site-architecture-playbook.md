# Bengaluru Technical SEO and Site Architecture Playbook

When local pages are structurally weak, rankings and conversion quality both suffer.

## Architecture essentials

- Clean route hierarchy for city and intent pages.
- Consistent metadata patterns with no duplicate titles/H1.
- JSON-LD for LocalBusiness, FAQPage, and BreadcrumbList.
- Internal links from hubs, services, and support blogs.

## Operating model

Ship in batches, validate in GSC, optimize CTR first, then optimize conversion.
