# Chennai Service Pages: Why Traffic Does Not Convert

Traffic without conversion is usually a page architecture problem, not a traffic problem.

## Why conversion fails

- Offer is unclear in above-fold copy.
- Proof and differentiation are buried.
- FAQ does not address buyer concerns.
- CTA language is generic.

## What to do now

1. Rewrite page promise around one intent.
2. Place proof before CTA.
3. Add local FAQ tied to objections in Chennai market.
