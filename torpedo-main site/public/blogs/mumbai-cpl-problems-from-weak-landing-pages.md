# Mumbai CPL Problems from Weak Landing Pages

Mumbai campaigns often fail because landing pages are not built for local commercial intent.

## Why CPL inflates

- Keyword intent and page promise are mismatched.
- Slow mobile experiences increase bounce.
- Proof sections are weak and unconvincing.
- CTA placement is inconsistent.

## Immediate fixes

1. Align page with one high-intent Mumbai query.
2. Prioritize mobile speed and CTA clarity.
3. Add city-specific proof and outcomes.
4. Test title/meta before redesigning page layout.
