WhatsApp is powerful for retargeting, but the channel punishes lazy strategy. Without clear consent and message relevance, you lose trust fast.

## Consent is a growth asset, not a legal checkbox
Explicit opt-in improves deliverability, response quality, and trust. Build consent at source: website forms, lead magnets, and ad flows should state message purpose and frequency.

## Segment before you send anything
Do not broadcast one script to all leads. Segment by stage, source, service interest, and recency. Retargeting works when timing matches buyer context.
> Insight Block: Retargeting quality rises when consent capture and segmentation design happen together.

## Build a three-step retargeting sequence
Step 1 reminder with value. Step 2 objection handling with proof. Step 3 clear next-step CTA such as consultation or pricing review.

## Coordinate WhatsApp with your funnel
WhatsApp should complement website, ads, and sales follow-up. Track message click-through and downstream meeting rate.

## Protect trust with frequency guardrails
Set message caps and quiet windows. Over-messaging creates churn and complaints, especially in local service categories.
> Insight Block: The best WhatsApp campaigns feel like timely help, not automated pressure.

## Actionable close
Audit opt-in sources and rewrite one underperforming sequence this week. Better sequencing often improves conversation quality without extra budget.
