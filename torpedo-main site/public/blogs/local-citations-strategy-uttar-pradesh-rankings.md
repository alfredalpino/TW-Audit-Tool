Submitting your business to 200 directories does not build local authority. Clean, consistent, and relevant citations do.

## What citations do in modern local SEO
Citations reinforce business legitimacy by validating name, address, and phone consistency across the web. They are trust multipliers, not standalone ranking hacks.

## Start with citation hygiene before expansion
Audit existing listings for duplicate profiles, old phone numbers, and formatting mismatch. Inconsistent NAP data confuses search engines and users, especially for multi-location brands.
> Insight Block: Citation consistency is a trust infrastructure task, not an SEO gimmick.

## Prioritize relevance over volume
Choose quality local and industry directories first. A few trusted citations often outperform mass submissions to low-quality portals.

## Connect citations to on-site signals
Ensure website contact data, location pages, and LocalBusiness schema align with citation records. Inconsistency can dilute listing value.

## Build an ongoing maintenance cadence
Citations are not one-time tasks. Ownership changes and branch shifts happen. Schedule quarterly citation reviews in your local SEO rhythm.
> Insight Block: In competitive UP categories, cleaner data quality can become a differentiator.

## External references

## Actionable close
Run a citation consistency audit for your top three locations and fix mismatches in one sprint to unlock visibility gains without extra ad spend.
