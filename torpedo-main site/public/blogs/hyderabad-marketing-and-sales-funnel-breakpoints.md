# Hyderabad Marketing and Sales Funnel Breakpoints

Many Hyderabad campaigns fail after click because page architecture and sales handoff are disconnected.

## Breakpoints

- Misaligned page promise and offer.
- Form friction on mobile.
- Weak trust proof for first-time buyers.
- No lead qualification signals for sales.

## Fix sequence

1. Improve query-to-page relevance.
2. Reduce conversion friction.
3. Add structured proof and FAQ.
4. Track qualified lead ratio weekly.
