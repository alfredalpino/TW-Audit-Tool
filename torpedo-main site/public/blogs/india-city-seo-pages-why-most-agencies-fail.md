# India City SEO Pages: Why Most Agencies Fail

Most agencies fail because they scale volume before they build a quality system.

## Failure patterns

- Thin location pages with swapped city names.
- No quality gates for metadata and schema.
- Poor internal link structure.
- No CTR and conversion testing loop.

## Better model

Treat location SEO as a product system with data contracts, validations, and iterative optimization.
