1.5 **Effective Date:** The date first written above.
1.6 **Intellectual Property Rights (IPR):** All intellectual property rights, including copyrights, patents, trademarks, trade secrets, and other proprietary rights.
1.7 **Services:** The digital engineering services to be performed by Service Provider for Client, as described in an SOW.
1.8 **Statement of Work (SOW):** A document executed by both Parties that describes the specific Services, Deliverables, timelines, and fees for a particular project, and which incorporates the terms of this Agreement.

## ARTICLE II: SERVICES AND STATEMENTS OF WORK
