# TorpedoWeb LLC: Internal Document Review & Redline Report

This report provides a critical review and redline suggestions for TorpedoWeb LLC's foundational documents, aligning them with Delaware corporate law and institutional-grade agency best practices.

## 1. LLC Operating Agreement

| Section | Current State | Redline Suggestion / Best Practice | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Governing Law** | Implicitly Delaware | **Explicitly state Delaware.** | Avoids ambiguity and ensures the "Contractual Freedom" of Delaware LLC law is fully leveraged. |
| **Dispute Resolution** | General litigation | **Add Mandatory Arbitration in Wilmington, DE.** | Reduces public exposure and litigation costs. Use AAA or JAMS. |
| **Member Duties** | Standard fiduciary duties | **Specify/Limit Fiduciary Duties.** | Delaware allows LLCs to modify or eliminate fiduciary duties (except the implied covenant of good faith and fair dealing) to protect managers. |
| **Electronic Signatures** | Not explicitly mentioned | **Add Electronic Signatures Clause.** | Essential for a remote-first team. Reference Delaware's UETA. |

## 2. Independent Contractor Agreement (ICA) & IP Assignment

| Section | Current State | Redline Suggestion / Best Practice | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **IP Ownership** | "Work made for hire" | **Add "Present Assignment" language.** | Use "Contractor *hereby assigns*" instead of "agrees to assign" to ensure immediate transfer of rights. |
| **Moral Rights** | Often omitted | **Explicitly waive Moral Rights.** | Critical for international contractors (e.g., in Europe) where moral rights are distinct from copyright. |
| **Classification** | Basic status statement | **Add robust "Independent Contractor" representations.** | Mitigates IRS misclassification risk. Contractor should represent they have their own equipment and other clients. |
| **Tax Compliance** | Vague | **Require W-9 (US) or W-8BEN (Intl) before payment.** | Ensures compliance with federal backup withholding rules. |

## 3. Master Services Agreement (MSA) & SOW

| Section | Current State | Redline Suggestion / Best Practice | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Liability Cap** | Often missing or unlimited | **Cap liability at fees paid in last 12 months.** | Standard protection for agencies to prevent catastrophic claims. |
| **Payment/Refunds** | Net 30 standard | **Add late fee and "no refund" for custom work.** | Protects cash flow and defines the bespoke nature of digital engineering. |
| **Termination** | Vague notice | **Add "Termination for Convenience" with wind-down fee.** | Allows both parties an exit while compensating the agency for the transition. |

## 4. Onboarding Checklists

| Item | Status | Suggestion |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Legal Review** | Missing | Add "Conflict of Interest" disclosure for all new contractors. |
| **Security** | Missing | Require 2FA on all company-provided or used accounts as a condition of engagement. |
| **Compliance** | Missing | Verify receipt and acknowledgment of the Information Security Policy. |

**Note:** Items marked as "High Severity" or "Critical" in the audit should be prioritized for immediate redline implementation.
