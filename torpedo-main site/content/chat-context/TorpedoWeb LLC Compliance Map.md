# TorpedoWeb LLC Compliance Map

This Compliance Map annotates the newly drafted Terms of Service (ToS) and Privacy Policy (PP) with references to relevant laws and regulations, demonstrating how specific clauses address legal requirements and best practices. This document is intended to provide a clear overview for legal and product teams.

## 1. Terms of Service Compliance Map

| ToS Clause | Relevant Laws/Regulations | Rationale/Coverage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **2. Acceptance of Terms** | General Contract Law (Delaware) | Establishes mutual assent and enforceability of the agreement. |
| **3. Use of the Services** | Federal/State Laws (e.g., CAN-SPAM Act, Computer Fraud and Abuse Act) | Prevents illegal activities, network abuse, and protects system integrity. |
| **4. Intellectual Property Rights** | 17 U.S.C. § 101 (Copyright Act), Delaware Contract Law | Defines ownership of IP, crucial for digital agencies. Ensures TorpedoWeb's and client's IP are protected. |
| **5. Payment, Refunds, and Termination** | Delaware Contract Law, UCC (Uniform Commercial Code) | Governs financial transactions, protects revenue, and provides clear exit strategies. |
| **6. Confidentiality and Trade Secret Protection** | 6 Del. C. § 2001 et seq. (DUTSA), 18 U.S.C. § 1836 et seq. (DTSA) | Protects proprietary information and methodologies, vital for a tech agency. |
| **7. DMCA / Acceptable Use** | 17 U.S.C. § 512 (DMCA), General Internet Content Laws | Provides DMCA safe harbor, prevents misuse of services, and protects against illegal content. |
| **8. Disclaimers and Limitation of Liability** | 6 Del. C. § 18-1101 (Delaware LLC Act), Delaware Contract Law | Limits financial exposure, disclaims implied warranties, and is standard in tech contracts. |
| **9. Indemnification** | Delaware Contract Law | Shifts risk to the party causing harm, protecting TorpedoWeb from third-party claims. |
| **10. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution** | 6 Del. C. § 18-1101 (Delaware LLC Act), Federal Arbitration Act | Ensures predictability in legal interpretation and provides efficient dispute resolution via arbitration in Delaware. |
| **11. Miscellaneous** | Delaware Contract Law | Standard boilerplate clauses for contract completeness and enforceability. |

## 2. Privacy Policy Compliance Map

| PP Clause | Relevant Laws/Regulations | Rationale/Coverage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Introduction** | GDPR Article 12, CCPA/CPRA § 1798.100 | Establishes transparency and commitment to privacy. |
| **2. Information We Collect** | GDPR Article 13, CCPA/CPRA § 1798.100 | Clearly defines categories of personal data collected. |
| **3. How We Collect Information** | GDPR Article 13 | Explains methods of data collection. |
| **4. Lawful Bases for Processing** | GDPR Article 6 | Explicitly states legal grounds for processing, mandatory for GDPR compliance. |
| **5. How We Use Your Information** | GDPR Article 13, CCPA/CPRA § 1798.100 | Details purposes of data use, aligning with lawful bases. |
| **6. How We Share Your Information** | GDPR Article 13, CCPA/CPRA § 1798.100 | Explains data sharing practices, including with service providers. |
| **7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies** | ePrivacy Directive (Cookie Law), GDPR Article 7 | Informs users about cookie usage and provides control options. |
| **8. Data Retention** | GDPR Article 5(1)(e), CCPA/CPRA § 1798.100 | Adheres to data minimization and storage limitation principles. |
| **9. Data Security and Breach Notification** | GDPR Articles 32-34, CCPA/CPRA § 1798.82 | Outlines security measures and breach notification procedures. |
| **10. Your Data Protection Rights** | GDPR Articles 15-22, CCPA/CPRA § 1798.100 et seq. | Informs users of their rights (access, rectification, erasure, etc.). |
| **11. International Data Transfers** | GDPR Chapter V | Addresses cross-border data transfers and safeguards (e.g., SCCs). |
| **12. Children's Privacy** | COPPA, GDPR Article 8 | States policy on children's data, disclaiming services for minors. |
| **13. HIPAA Flags (If Applicable)** | HIPAA | Clarifies that PHI is not handled, avoiding unintended compliance burdens. |
| **14. Changes to This Privacy Policy** | GDPR Article 13 | Ensures users are informed of policy updates. |
| **15. Contact Us** | GDPR Article 13 | Provides clear contact for privacy inquiries. |

## 3. Data Processing Addendum (DPA) Compliance Map

| DPA Clause | Relevant Laws/Regulations | Rationale/Coverage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Definitions** | GDPR Article 4, CCPA/CPRA | Aligns terminology with key data protection laws. |
| **2. Processing of Personal Data** | GDPR Article 28 | Defines roles (Controller/Processor) and details of processing. |
| **3. Processor's Obligations** | GDPR Article 28, CCPA/CPRA | Outlines Processor's duties: instructions, confidentiality, security, sub-processing, data subject rights, breach notification, DPIA assistance, data deletion/return. |
| **4. Controller's Obligations** | GDPR Article 28 | Defines Controller's responsibilities: lawful processing, data subject consent. |
| **5. International Data Transfers** | GDPR Chapter V | Specifies mechanisms for international data transfers (e.g., SCCs). |
| **6. Audits** | GDPR Article 28(3)(h) | Ensures Controller's right to audit Processor's compliance. |
| **7. Limitation of Liability** | Delaware Contract Law | Links DPA liability to the main Agreement's limitations. |
| **8. Governing Law** | Delaware Contract Law | Establishes governing law for the DPA. |
