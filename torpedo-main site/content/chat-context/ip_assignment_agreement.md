# INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement (this "Agreement") is made and entered into as of [Date], by and between:

**TorpedoWeb LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company, with its principal place of business at [TorpedoWeb Address] ("Company"); and

**[Assignor Name]**, an individual residing at [Assignor Address] or a [Assignor Entity Type] with its principal place of business at [Assignor Address] ("Assignor").

Company and Assignor are hereinafter collectively referred to as the "Parties" and individually as a "Party."

## RECITALS

A. Assignor has developed or will develop certain intellectual property for Company in connection with [Description of Services or Project, e.g., software development services pursuant to an Independent Contractor Agreement dated [Date]].

B. Company desires to acquire all right, title, and interest in and to such intellectual property, and Assignor desires to assign such intellectual property to Company.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

## ARTICLE I: ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

1.1 **Assignment:** Assignor hereby irrevocably and unconditionally assigns, transfers, and conveys to Company, and its successors and assigns, effective as of the date of creation of each item of Assigned IP, all right, title, and interest in and to all intellectual property created, conceived, or developed by Assignor, either alone or with others, in the course of performing services for Company, including, but not limited to, software code, designs, algorithms, documentation, inventions, discoveries, improvements, trade secrets, copyrights, trademarks, and all other proprietary rights (collectively, the "Assigned IP"). This assignment is a present assignment of existing and future Assigned IP and operates as an assignment of rights in Assigned IP as and when created.

1.2 **Work Made for Hire:** To the extent that any of the Assigned IP may be considered a "work made for hire" under applicable copyright law (17 U.S.C. § 101), it is hereby agreed that such Assigned IP shall be a work made for hire for Company. To the extent that any Assigned IP is not deemed a work made for hire, Assignor hereby assigns all right, title, and interest in and to such Assigned IP to Company as provided in Section 1.1.

1.3 **Moral Rights Waiver (for International Assignors):** To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, Assignor hereby waives any and all moral rights, including rights of attribution and integrity, with respect to the Assigned IP. Assignor agrees not to assert any moral rights against Company or its successors or assigns.

## ARTICLE II: FURTHER ASSURANCES

2.1 **Further Assurances:** Assignor agrees to execute any documents and take any actions reasonably requested by Company, at Company’s expense, to perfect Company’s ownership of the Assigned IP and all Intellectual Property Rights therein, including, without limitation, assisting Company in obtaining and enforcing patents, copyrights, and other proprietary rights in the Assigned IP. These obligations survive termination of any underlying service agreement and remain in effect until Company’s ownership is fully perfected. Assignor hereby irrevocably appoints Company as Assignor’s attorney-in-fact to execute and file any such documents in Assignor’s name if Assignor fails to do so within fifteen (15) days of Company’s request.

## ARTICLE III: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

3.1 **Assignor Representations:** Assignor represents and warrants that:
    a. Assignor is the sole owner of all right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP prior to its assignment to Company;
    b. Assignor has the full right and authority to enter into this Agreement and to assign the Assigned IP to Company;
    c. The Assigned IP does not infringe upon the Intellectual Property Rights of any third party;
    d. Assignor has not previously assigned, transferred, or encumbered any rights in the Assigned IP inconsistent with this Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV: CONFIDENTIALITY

4.1 **Confidential Information:** Assignor acknowledges that the Assigned IP and any information related to its development constitutes Confidential Information of Company. Assignor agrees to keep all such Confidential Information strictly confidential and not to disclose or use such Confidential Information for any purpose other than for the benefit of Company.

## ARTICLE V: GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

5.1 **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflicts of laws principles.

5.2 **Arbitration:** THE PARTIES WAIVE ANY RIGHT TO A JURY TRIAL AND AGREE THAT ANY PROCEEDING SHALL BE CONDUCTED ON AN INDIVIDUAL BASIS AND NOT IN A CLASS, CONSOLIDATED, OR REPRESENTATIVE ACTION. Any dispute, controversy, or claim arising out of or relating to this Agreement, or the breach, termination, or invalidity thereof, shall be settled by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association (AAA) in accordance with its Commercial Arbitration Rules. The place of arbitration shall be Wilmington, Delaware. The arbitration shall be conducted by a single arbitrator. The arbitration and all related proceedings shall be kept confidential by the Parties and the arbitrator except as required by law or to enforce an award. The award rendered by the arbitrator shall be final and binding upon the Parties, and judgment thereon may be entered in any court of competent jurisdiction in the State of Delaware. The Parties consent to exclusive jurisdiction and venue in the state and federal courts located in Wilmington, Delaware for any action to enforce an award or for any proceeding not subject to arbitration.

## ARTICLE VI: MISCELLANEOUS

6.1 **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written.

6.2 **Amendments:** No amendment or modification of this Agreement shall be effective unless in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both Parties.

6.3 **Severability:** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

6.4 **Electronic Signatures:** This Agreement may be executed by electronic signature, which shall be considered as an original signature for all purposes.
